DesignPatterns (DPs)
CP650 - Lec 3 33
• Principles and idiomatic solutions built by experienced 
OO developers to guide in the creation of software. 
• In OO Design, a pattern is a named description of a 
problem and solution that can be applied to new 
contexts 
• Ideally a pattern advises on how to apply its solution 
in varying circumstances and considers the forces and 
trade-offs
• Patternsoftenareassociatedwithsoftwarecomponents,for 
example,GitHub or platform websites

Design Patterns (DPs)
• Best practice : Use patterns each time it’s possible.
• Bad practice : Use patterns everywhere and for 
everything => Lead to over architecture or unbalanced 
and inefficient code
• Patterns generally lead to:
• More general design, More adaptability
• Less maintainable architecture
• You have to analyze and always find the good tradeoff 
between genericity and specialization.
